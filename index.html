    How does This App Work?

    • Create a Message

      • Open the FutureSend app and select whether you want to send an SMS or an email.
      • Enter the recipient's phone number or email address.
      • Type your message or choose from pre-defined templates for quick communication.

    • Schedule Your Message

      • Set the date and time for when you want your message to be sent.
      • Review the details and confirm the schedule.

    • Automatic Delivery

      • FutureSend will automatically send your SMS or email at the scheduled time.
      • You can view, edit, or cancel scheduled messages before they are sent..
