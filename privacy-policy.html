Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [12/02/2025]

At Futuresend, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our mobile app, Futuresend ("the App").

1. Information We Collect We may collect the following types of information when you use Futuresend:

Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other personal identifiers you provide when creating an account or contacting customer support. Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interaction with the App, such as device type, operating system, app version, pages viewed, and the time you spent using the app. Location Data: If you enable location services on your device, we may collect your geolocation data to enhance your experience within the App. Payment Information: If you make purchases through the App, we may collect billing information, payment method, and transaction details. We use third-party services to process payments, and they handle this information in accordance with their privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To provide, maintain, and improve the App. To personalize your experience and respond to your inquiries or requests. To process payments, if applicable. To send you notifications, updates, and promotional offers (if you have opted-in). To monitor and analyze usage trends to enhance the App’s functionality and user experience. To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and safety.

3. How We Share Your Information We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following situations:

With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate and improve the App, such as payment processors, data hosting services, and analytics providers. These providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was shared. For Legal Compliance: We may share your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect the rights and safety of others, or prevent fraud or unlawful activities. With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you provide us with explicit consent to do so.

4. Data Security We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you. Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data. Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., when we are required to retain it for legal or security reasons). Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the communication or adjusting your preferences in the App settings. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [Insert Contact Email].

6. Children's Privacy Futuresend is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as soon as possible. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Links The App may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you within the App or through other means. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
